About Me

Course: Welding and Fabrication

Attachment Period: 4 May – 31 August

Company: Olenguruone Tea Factory

Industrial Supervisor: John Siele

Week 2

Monday: Today I continued with material preparation for fabrication work. I measured, marked, and cut mild steel square tubes according to the dimensions provided in the working drawing. After cutting, I checked each piece for accuracy and removed burrs using an angle grinder. Tuesday: Today I assisted in fabricating a metal stand. I arranged the cut steel members on a flat surface and checked the alignment using a try square. After confirming the measurements, I tack welded the joints before final welding. Wednesday: Today I practiced MIG welding by checking shielding gas, adjusting wire feed speed and voltage, and producing clean welds. Thursday: Today I carried out grinding and finishing of fabricated products and inspected weld quality. Friday: Today I participated in routine maintenance of workshop equipment and cleaned the workshop.
